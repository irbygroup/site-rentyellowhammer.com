# Figma Design — Luxury Event Venue Website (1 of 3 Venues, Reusable Template)

We're Yellowhammer Hospitality, a boutique event venue company in Mobile, Alabama with 3 properties. We need a talented designer to create a complete Figma design system for our venue websites — starting with **The Courtyard on Dauphin** as the template that will be replicated across all 3 venues.

## What We Need

A complete, pixel-perfect Figma design for a luxury event venue website with **8 pages + 3 ad landing pages**, designed at desktop, tablet, and mobile breakpoints. This design will be handed off to a Next.js developer for implementation.

## Pages to Design

1. **Home** — Hero video/imagery area, prominent "Book a Tour" CTA, phone number (251-333-RENT), venue highlights, testimonials/reviews
2. **The Venue** — About, location, history, photo highlights, what makes us unique
3. **Weddings** — Dedicated landing page targeting brides/wedding planners (will be used for Google & Facebook ads)
4. **Parties & Personal Events** — Landing page for baby showers, birthday parties, anniversaries, reunions, etc. (ad landing page)
5. **Corporate Events** — Landing page for corporate retreats, meetings, team building, holiday parties (ad landing page)
6. **Photo Gallery** — Grid/lightbox gallery layout showcasing the venue and past events
7. **Pricing** — Package overview with lead capture form (no public pricing — goal is to capture the lead)
8. **Contact** — Lead capture form, phone, email, Google Maps embed, hours

## Design Requirements

- **"Book a Tour" is the #1 CTA** — should be prominent on every page, in the header/nav, floating on mobile
- **251-333-RENT phone number** prominent throughout (especially mobile)
- **Reviews/testimonials** integrated on all pages (not just a dedicated reviews page)
- **Lead capture forms** on Pricing and Contact pages (name, email, phone, event type, date, message)
- **3 responsive breakpoints:** Desktop (1440px), Tablet (768px), Mobile (375px)
- **Component-based design** — reusable header, footer, CTA blocks, testimonial cards, gallery grid, form components, etc. organized in a Figma component library so the developer can build a clean design system
- **Ad landing pages** (Weddings, Parties, Corporate) should have clear conversion funnels — hero, social proof, features/benefits, strong CTA, minimal navigation distractions

## Design Direction

- **Clean, elegant, modern** — lots of whitespace, beautiful typography
- Think high-end boutique hotel or luxury wedding venue, NOT generic event template
- Let the photography do the heavy lifting — large hero images, full-bleed photo sections
- Warm and inviting but professional
- The Courtyard on Dauphin is a historic downtown venue with a charming courtyard, wrought iron gates, and exposed brick — the design should complement that aesthetic
- Look at top-tier venue sites for inspiration (The Knot featured venues, Junebug Weddings, etc.)
