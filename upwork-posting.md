# Next.js Event Venue Website — Phase 1: Courtyard on Dauphin (1 of 3 Venues)

We're Yellowhammer Hospitality, a boutique event venue company in Mobile, Alabama with 3 properties. We need a developer to build clean, modern, SEO-optimized Next.js websites for each venue — starting with **The Courtyard on Dauphin** (courtyardondauphin.com) as Phase 1.

## The Big Picture

We have 4 domains:
- **rentyellowhammer.com** — Corporate hub linking to all 3 venues
- **courtyardondauphin.com** — The Courtyard on Dauphin (Phase 1)
- **oakandfountain.com** — Oak & Fountain (Phase 2)
- **hallettirby.com** — The Hallett-Irby House (Phase 2)

All 3 venue sites will share the same template/theme/design and cross-link to each other. Phase 1 is designing and building the Courtyard site to 100% completion. Phase 2 (separate contract) will replicate it for the other 2 venues and build the corporate landing page at rentyellowhammer.com.

## Phase 1 Scope — courtyardondauphin.com

Pages needed:
1. **Home** — Hero imagery/video, prominent "Book a Tour" CTA, phone number (251-333-RENT), venue highlights, reviews
2. **The Venue** — About, location, history, photo gallery, what makes us unique
3. **Weddings** — Dedicated landing page targeting wedding searches (SEO + Google/Facebook ad landing page)
4. **Parties & Personal Events** — Landing page targeting baby showers, birthday parties, anniversaries, reunions, etc.
5. **Corporate Events** — Landing page targeting corporate retreats, meetings, team building, holiday parties, etc.
6. **Photo Gallery** — Grid/lightbox gallery showcasing the venue and past events
7. **Pricing** — Overview of packages with lead capture form (no public pricing — goal is to capture the lead)
8. **Contact** — Lead capture form, phone, email, Google Maps embed, hours

## Key Requirements

- **Tech stack:** Next.js (App Router), Tailwind CSS, deployed on Vercel
- **"Book a Tour" is the #1 CTA** — prominent on every page, in the header, floating on mobile, etc.
- **251-333-RENT phone number** prominent throughout (click-to-call on mobile)
- **Lead capture forms** on Pricing and Contact pages (name, email, phone, event type, date, message)
- **Chatwoot** live chat widget integrated
- **Reviews/testimonials** displayed on all pages
- **SEO-first:** Clean semantic HTML, proper heading hierarchy, meta titles/descriptions, Open Graph tags, JSON-LD structured data for LocalBusiness/EventVenue, fast Core Web Vitals
- **3 ad landing pages** (Weddings, Parties, Corporate) optimized for Google PPC and Facebook ad traffic with clear conversion paths
- **Mobile-first responsive design** — clean, elegant, modern. Think high-end boutique hotel, not generic event venue template
- **Fast** — target 90+ Lighthouse scores across the board

## Design Direction

- Clean, elegant, modern — lots of whitespace, beautiful typography
- Let the photography do the heavy lifting
- Warm and inviting but professional
- Color palette should complement the venue's aesthetic (historic downtown Mobile courtyard setting)
